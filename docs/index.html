Dev Bones is a #12in12 side project bashed together in under a month by Kez. Many bones grave robbed from the HTML5 Boilerplate and mrmrs HTML template. No claims as to perfection and efficiency, this is based on personal experience with simple builds and put out to the internet for free to help others get their next zombie started.
